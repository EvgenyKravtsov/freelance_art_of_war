Colossal: A model fighting with this weapon Pile In and fights at Initiative step 1.

Hurl: If an Imperial Knight fighting with a Thunderstrike Gauntlet destroys an enemy Monstrous Creature or vehicle in the Fight sub-phase, it can choose to hurl it (Gargantuan Creatures, Super-heavy vehicles and buildings cannot be hurled). If a vehicle was destroyed as a result of suffering an Explodes! result on the Vehicle Damage table, resolve any damage before hurling it. Any passengers must take an emergency disembarkation before their transport vehicle is hurled. To hurl an enemy model, immediately resolve a shooting attack against an enemy unit within 12" that is not locked in combat using profile below. A hurled model is removed from the battlefield after the attack has been resolved.
