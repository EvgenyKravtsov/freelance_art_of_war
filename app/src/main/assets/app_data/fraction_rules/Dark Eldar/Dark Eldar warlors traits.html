When generating their Warlord Traits, a Dark Eldar Warlord may either roll on one of the Warlord Traits tables in Warhammer 40,000: The Rules, or roll on the table below.

D6 Warlord Trait

1. Ancient Evil. The Warlord, and all friendly units with the Dark Eldar Faction within 12" of the Warlord, have the Fear special rule.

2. Labyrinthine Cunning. The Dark Eldar player can re-roll the dice when they attempt to Seize the Initiative, determine if the Night Fighting rules are in effect, and when they make Reserve Rolls.

3. Soulthirst. The Warlord has the Rage special rule.

4. Hatred Eternal. The Warlord has the Hatred special rule.

5. Blood Dancer. The Warlord receives +1 to the Weapon Skill characteristic on his profile.

6. Towering Arrogance. The Warlord, and all friendly units with the Dark Eldar Faction within 12" of the Warlord, have the Fearless special rule.
