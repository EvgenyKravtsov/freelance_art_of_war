Warlord Traits

1. Born of Sorcery

Your Warlord harnesses Warp Charge points on the result of 3+.

2. Incorporeal Form

Enemy models must reduce their Weapon Skill and Ballistic Skill characteristics by 1 (to a minimum of 1) when targeting your Warlord or his unit.

3. Warp Tether

Add 1 to the invulnerable save of your Warlord and all friendly units with the Daemon of Tzeentch special rule within 9" of him.

4. Lorekeeper of Tzeentch

Add 1 to your Warlord’s Mastery Level. If your Warlord is not a Psyker, he instead has the Psyker (Mastery Level 1) special rule and generates his powers from the Discipline of Change.

5. Tyrant of the Warp

Your Warlord ignores the first Perils of the Warp he suffers during each Psychic phase.

6. Daemonspark

The close combat attacks of your Warlord, and all friendly units with the Daemon of Tzeentch special rule whilst they are within 9" of him, gain the Soul Blaze special rule.
