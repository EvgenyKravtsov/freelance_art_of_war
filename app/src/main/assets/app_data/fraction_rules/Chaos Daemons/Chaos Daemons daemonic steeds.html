Only Infrantry models may choose daemonic steeds, and a model may only take a daemonic steed if it also has the Mark of the appropriate Chaos God.

Juggernaut of Khorne: The model receives +1 Toughness, +1 Wound, +1 Attack and his unit type becomes Cavalry.

Disc of Tzeentch: The model receives +1 Attack and his unit type becomes Jetbike.

Palanquin of Nurgle: The model receives +2 Wounds, +1 Attack and gains the Very Bulky special rule.

Steed of Slaanesh: The model receives +1 Attack and gains the Acute Senses and Outflank special rules. Furthermore, their unit type becomes Cavalry.
