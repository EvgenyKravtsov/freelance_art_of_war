1. HYSTERICAL FRENZY ............................. WARP CHARGE I

Hysterical Frenzy is a blessing that targets a single friendly non-vehicle unit within 12". The target unit rolls on the table below to determine what benefit all models in the unit gain whilst the power is in effect.

D3  Disease Granted
1 Swollen Sensorium: +1 Initiative.
2 Lunatic Strength: + 1 Strength.
3 Hyperactive Fit: +1 Attack.

2. DELIGHTFUL AGONIES .............................WARP CHARGE 1

Delightful Agonies is a blessing with a range of 12". Whilst the power is in effect, all models in the target unit have the Feel No Pain (4+) special rule.

3. SYMPHONY OF PAIN ............................. WARP CHARGE 1

Symphony of Pain is a malediction that targets a single enemy unit within 24". Whilst this power is in effect, that unit is at -1 Weapon Skill and -1 Ballistic Skill. Furthermore, any Attacks from sonic weaponry that hit the target unit whilst this power is in effect are resolved at +1 Strength. Note that the effects of more than one Symphony of Pain are cumulative.
