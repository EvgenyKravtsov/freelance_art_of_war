Warlord Traits

1. Insensate to Pain

Add 1 to your Warlord's Feel No Pain rolls.

2. Tainted Regeneration

Your Warlord has the It Will Not Die special rule.

3. Lord Contagion

Your Warlord and all friendly units from a Death Guard Formation or Detachment that are within 7" of your Warlord at the start of the Fight sub-phase are infected with contagious plague until the end of the phase. All enemy units that are locked in combat with any units infected in this manner suffer D6 Strength 1 AP 4 hits with the Poisoned (4+) special rule (distributed randomly) at the Initiative 10 step.

4. Hulking Physique

Add 1 to your Warlord's Wounds characteristic.

5. Rotten Constitution

Your Warlord has the Ethernal Warrior special rule.

6. Arch-contaminator

The effect of the Poisoned special rule on any weapons wielded by your Warlord and any models in his unit is improved by 1, to a maximum of 2+. For example, if your Warlord joined a unit of Plague Marines, their plague knives would have the Poisoned (3+) special rule instead of Poisoned (4+).
