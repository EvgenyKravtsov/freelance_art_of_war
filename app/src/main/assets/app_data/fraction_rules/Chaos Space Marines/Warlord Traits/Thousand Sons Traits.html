Warlord Traits

1. Arrogance of Aeons

Your Warlord has the Adamantium Will special rule and can choose to re-roll a single dice each time they make Deny the Witch test.

2. Undying Form

Your Warlord has the Ethernal Warrior special rule.

3. Aetherstride

Your Warlord and his unit are not slowed by difficult terrain and do not suffer the penalty to their Initiative for charging through difficult terrain.

4. Lord of Forbidden Lore

Your Warlord knows one additional psychic power.

5. Walker of the Webway

Your Warlord and his unit have the Deep Strike special rule. If they already had the Deep Strike special rule, then do not roll for scatter when they arrive by Deep Strike.

6. Lord of Flux

Enemy units within 12" of your Warlord treat all terrrain, even open ground, as difficult terrain. In addition, all models in any enemy units that Run, Turbo-boost, move Flat Out or charge within 12" of your Warlord must take a Dangerous Terrain test.
