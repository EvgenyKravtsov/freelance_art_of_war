DAEMONIC STEEDS

Only Infantry models may choose daemonic steeds, and a model may only take a dacmonic steed if it also has the Mark of the appropriate Chaos God.

Juggernaut of Khorne:

The model receives + 1 Toughness, + 1 Wound, + 1 Attack and his unit type becomes Cavalry (see the Warhammer 40,000 rule book for details).

Disc of Tzeentch:

The model receives +1 Attack and his unit type becomes Jetbike (see the Warhammer 40,000 rule book for details).

Palanquin of Nurgle:

The model receives +2 Wounds, +1 Attack and gains the Very Bulky special rule.

Steed of Slaanesh:

The model receives +1 Attack, and adds +3" to his Run moves. Furthermore, he gains the Acute Senses and Outflank special rules, and his unit type becomes Cavalry (see the Warhammer 40,000 rule book for details).
