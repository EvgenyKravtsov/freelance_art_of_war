CHAOS ICONS

When determining assault results, add one to your total if there are one or more friendly units with a Chaos Icon locked in that combat. Chaos Icons also have additional effects, which are listed below.

ICON OF WRATH

Units with the Mark of Khorne only. All models with the Mark of Khorne in a unit equipped with an icon of wrath have the Furious Charge special rule. When determining its charge range, a unit with this icon may re-roll the result.

ICON OF FLAME

Units with the Mark of Tzeentch only. All boltguns, combibolters, heavy bolters and bolt pistols carried by models with the Mark of Tzeentch in a unit equipped with an icon of flame have the Soul Blaze special rule.

ICON OF DESPAIR

Units with the Mark of Nurgle only. All models with the Mark of Nurgle in a unit equipped with an icon of despair have the Fear special rule.

ICON OF EXCESS

Units with the Mark of Slaanesh only. All models with the Mark of Slaanesh in a unit equipped with an icon of excess have the Feel no Pain special rule.

ICON OF VENGEANCE

All models in a unit equipped with an icon of vengeance have the Fearless special rule.
