NIGHT LORDS SPECIAL RULES

Any Detachment with the Chaos Space Marines Faction
can be from one of the nine Traitor Legions. A Night Lords
Detachment retains the Chaos Space Marines Faction and is
treated in all ways as a Chaos Space Marines Detachment, with
the following modications:
• They cannot include any Unique units.
• Units that have a Mark of Chaos cannot be taken.
• Units cannot be upgraded to take any Marks of Chaos.
• All units that can do so must be upgraded to Veterans of the
Long War, at no additional points cost.
• Any Daemon of Tzeentch, Nurgle or Slaanesh that is a Psyker
can choose to generate all of their psychic powers from the
Discipline of Tzeentch, Nurgle or Slaanesh respectively.

All units in a Night Lords Detachment or Formation gain the
following special rules:

IN MIDNIGHT CLAD

Units that have the Veterans of the Long War special rule have
the Fear, Night Vision and Stealth special rules.

SKYBORNEHORROR

Units of Raptors are Troops choices instead of Fast
Attack choices.

TERROR TACTICS

Enemy units that are locked in combat with any units from a
Night Lords Detachment or Formation must subtract 2 from
their Leadership when taking Fear tests.
