Gifts of Chogoris

The Banner of the Eagle………...30 POINTS

May only be taken by model that may take items from the Space Marine Standarts list. Friendly White Scars units within 12” of a model with the Banner of the Eagle have the Fleet and Furious Charge special rules.

The Glaive of Vengeance…...30 POINTS

Range S AP Type
- +3/+1* 2/3* Melee, Master-crafted

* The Glaive of Vengeance has two profiles for both Strength and AP. The first is used only on a turn in which the bearer charged; the second is used at all other times.

The Hunter’s Eye……...20 POINTS

A model equippes with the Hunter’s Eye adds one to his Ballistic Skill and all his ranged weapons have the Ignores Cover special rule. Whilst a model eqquipped with the Hunter’s Eye is part of a unit, all other ranged weapons carries by models in that also have the Ignores Cover special rule.

Mantle of the Stormseer…..20 POINTS

Librarian only. A model eqquipped with the Mantle of the Stormseer has the Adamantium Will special rule and knows the Psychic Maelstrom power from the Telekinesis discipline in addition to any others he knows. This is not considered when determining if he has Psychic Focus.

Scimitar of the Great Khan…..25 POINTS

Range S AP Type
- +1 3 Melee, Warblessed, Master-crafted

Warblessed: When a model carrying this weapon fights in a challenge, add 3 to its Weapon Skill.

Wrath ot the Heavens…...25 POINTS

Wrath of the Heavens is a Space Marine bike, and cannot be taken by models equipped with Terminator armour or a jump pack. When a model riding Wrath of the Heavens Turbo-boosts, it can move up to 18” and pass over units and terrain as though it were a Jetbike.
