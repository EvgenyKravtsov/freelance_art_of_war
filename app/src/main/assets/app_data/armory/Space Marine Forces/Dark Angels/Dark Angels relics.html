Relics:

These artefacts are items of incredible rarity, ancient heirlooms that are maintained in places of honour on the Rock. Only the mightiest of Dark Angels are worthy to use such storied items, and their heroics and deeds simply add to the continuing legends and myths of the items themselves. Only one of each of the following relics may be chosen per army - there is but one of each of these items in the galaxy!
